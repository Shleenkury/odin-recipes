spaghetti

Ingredients

  • spaghetti sauce
  • parmesan cheese
  • ground beef w/salt and pepper
  • spaghetti noodles

Directions

  1. roll seasoned ground beef into meatballs
  2. cook covered in a pan with olive oil for 10 minutes
  3. bring a pot of water to a boil
  4. break noodles then toss them into the pot
  5. lower heat then pour in your spaghetti sauce onto the meatballs and let simmer
  6. stir pot of noodles until they're soft
  7. drain pot then place noodles on a plate
  8. pour spaghetti sauce with meatballs onto the noodles then sprinkle parmesan cheese onto it
