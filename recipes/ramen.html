ramen


A recipe for a delicious oriental dish, fit for anyone from any background or upbringing.
Even if you randomly happen to be, say, an Admiral from the hit anime ONE PIECE you'll love this dish!

Ingredients

  • Maruchan ramen packet (exclude the flavor packet)
  • garlic and ginger
  • Chicken broth
  • dried shiitake mushrooms
  • carrots
  • boiled egg

Instructions

  1. Stir-fry your garlic and ginger
  2. add chicken broth and dried shiitake mushrooms to a pot
  3. cook your noodles right in the broth
  4. add carrots
  5. add a boiled egg and pour in your garlic and ginger
  6. stir until noodles are soft

Enjoy, Admiral Kizaru from One Piece
