caesar salad


a classic, tasty salad and a healthy option for almost anyone trying to lose weight or build muscle.

INGREDIENTS

  • romaine lettuce
  • parmesan cheese
  • croutons
  • caesar dressing
  • cooked chicken breast
  • half a cup of olive oil
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1/2 tbsp of pepper
  • 1/2 tbsp of garlic powder

Steps

  1. butterfly a chicken breast and season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder
  2. fill a stainless steel pan with olive oil and heat it until the oil shimmers
  3. place the chicken breast on the pan and cook until the edges turn opaque
  4. flip the chicken breast and cook until golden brown on both sides or until internal temp is 165 degrees
  5. let chicken breast rest for 10 minutes
  6. break apart a head of romaine lettuce on a dish
  7. grate a block of parmesan cheese evenly around the dish
  8. evenly distribute four fingers full of croutons around the dish
  9. after resting, take the butterflied chicken breast and slice it in half
  10. put one half in the fridge for seconds, take the other half and dice it
  11. spread the diced chicken breast evenly around the dish
  12. finally, pour your caesar dressing onto the salad and toss it well.

    13. Enjoy Nigga
